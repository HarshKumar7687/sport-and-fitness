Weight Loss Workouts & Yoga

Weight Loss Workouts

  • HIIT Workout

    High-Intensity Interval Training is a great way to burn fat in a short amount of time.

  • Cardio Workout

    Steady-state cardio like running or cycling helps in burning calories.

  • Strength Training

    Building muscle helps increase your metabolism and burn fat.

Yoga

  • Sun Salutation (Surya Namaskar)

    A series of 12 powerful yoga poses that provide a great cardiovascular workout.

  • Warrior Pose (Virabhadrasana)

    This pose strengthens your legs and core while helping you improve balance.

  • Downward Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana)

    This foundational pose stretches your entire body and builds strength.
