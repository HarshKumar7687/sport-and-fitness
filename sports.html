CRICKET
Cricket is a bat-and-ball sport played between two teams of 11 players. The objective is to score more runs than the opposing team. One team bats, aiming to score runs by hitting the ball, while the other bowls and fields, trying to dismiss the batsmen and limit runs. Matches are played in various formats, including Test (5 days), One Day Internationals (50 overs), and Twenty20 (20 overs). The team with the most runs wins.
Cricket was originated in Britain.
Biggest League of Cricket is World Cup.

KABADDI
Kabaddi is a popular contact sport. It involves two teams, each trying to score points by sending a "raider" into the opponent's half to tag or wrestle with members of the opposing team before returning to their own half. The challenge for the raider is to do this while holding their breath and continuously chanting the word "kabaddi." If the raider is caught and unable to return, they are out. The defending team scores by stopping the raider.
Kabaddi originated from India.

HOCKEY
Hockey is a fast-paced team sport played on ice (ice hockey) or field (field hockey). It involves two teams trying to score goals by hitting a puck (ice) or ball (field) into the opponent's net using sticks. In ice hockey, players wear skates and protective gear due to the physical nature of the game. Ice hockey is most popular in countries like Canada, the U.S., and Russia, while field hockey is played widely in Europe, Asia, and Africa. Both versions require speed, strategy, and teamwork to succeed.
Ice Hockey was originated in Canada.
Field Hockey was Originated in England.

VOLLEY-BALL
Volleyball is a team sport where two teams of six players each try to score points by sending a ball over a net and into the opponent's court. Players use their hands and arms to hit the ball, and the team must prevent the ball from touching the ground on their side of the court. The game is played in sets, and the team that wins the most sets wins the match.
Volleyball was invented in the United States.

TENNIS
Tennis is a sport where players use rackets to hit a ball over a net and into the opponent's court. The game can be played individually (singles) or with a partner (doubles). Points are scored when the opponent fails to return the ball within the designated court area. Tennis is played on various surfaces, including grass, clay, and hard courts. The player who wins the most sets, determined by winning games, wins the match.
Tennis was originated in France.
Main event for tennis players is Olympics.

BASE-BALL
Baseball is a sport where two teams of nine players each compete to score runs by hitting a pitched ball and running around four bases arranged in a diamond. The game is played over nine innings, with each team taking turns to bat and field. The team with the most runs at the end of the game wins. Key elements include hitting, pitching, and fielding, with the goal of making it around the bases to score runs.
Baseball originates from United States Of America.
